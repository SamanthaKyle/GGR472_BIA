Kew Garden Stroll

A route for those who enjoy outdoor physical activity, play and tranquility. This trail takes travellers through Kew Gardens Park, a staple place of gathering and play in The Beaches. Individuals and families may explore the park's gazebo, wildlife, and flora, while children are sure to love the playground nested just south of Queen Street East.


While exploring, you may find:

Eastern Grey Squirrel (Sciurus carolinensis), Peregrine Falcon (Falco peregrinus), and Monarch (Danaus plexippus)


Note:

The purple heat-map present on the map represents areas of high biodiversity.

Ivan Forrest/Glen Stewart Hike

A trail for nature enthusiasts, hikers and those who enjoy a challenge. This trail winds through The Beaches Neighbourhood's popular Ivan Forrest Gardens and Glen Stewart Ravine. Whether it is the diversity in wildlife and flora or the steep elevation changes, the challenging trail is best suited for those up for a challenge. Experience all the ravine has to offer, and don't be afraid to go off the wooden path.


While exploring, you may find:

American Robin (Turdus migratorius), Turkey-Tail (Trametes versicolor), and Cooper's Hawk (Astur cooperii)


Note:

The purple heat-map present on the map represents areas of high biodiversity.

Pub Crawl

A route for those who enjoy exploring local bars and restaurants. This route is best for individuals and groups 19+. Experience what The Beaches has to offer as you meander along Queen Street East, stopping at exciting pubs throughout your journey. Don't be afraid to try a spot that catches your eye.


On your journey, you will visit:

The Stone Lion, The Wolfe Tone, Castro's Lounge, The Druid, The Outrigger, and The Hideout on the Beach

Art Walk

A route for parents in need of a fun night out. This route allows parents to drop off their kids at a fun art studio. From there, parents will meander along Queen Street, stopping at the many art stores and galleries the neighbourhood has to offer. Why not get a scoop of ice cream on your way too?


On your journey, you will visit:

Thought Fox Art Studio, Studio 88 Art Gallery, the Boardwalk, Beaches N' Cream, The Gardener's Cottage - Kew Williams House, and Incurable Collector

Date Night

Looking to explore the best of The Beaches? This route is for pairs seeking a memorable evening out! Page to Screen

A route for those looking to read a book, watch a movie, and explore The Beaches. This route is primarily for those who enjoy time with themselves or with a friend, and some quiet. Travellers will first start at The Beaches branch of The Toronto Public Library, picking out a book of their choosing. They may also want to check out the Coles bookstore just nextdoor. After, travellers will be encouraged to read their new book, before walking towards one of the oldest movie theatres in Toronto, The Fox Theatre. Travellers shouldn't be afraid to check out some stores along the way.


While exploring, you may find:

Toronto Public Library - Beaches Branch, Kew Gardens, Ivan Forrest Gardens, Judy's Beach Cafe, and The Fox Theatre

Pup Crawl

Take this route if you want to give your pup the ultimate day out! Start your adventure with a quick trip to a pet food store and grab a treat for your furry pal. Next, take a walk down to Kew Gardens Dog Park for some sandy paws and splashes in the lake. Finally, end the day by pampering the pooch at a doggy spa for a quick wash.


On your journey, you will visit:

RAW 101, Kew Gardens Dog Park, & Petninsula.
